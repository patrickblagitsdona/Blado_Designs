About

PATRICK

Blagits Dona

My name is Patrick Blagits Dona, I am a Brazilian Graphic Designer who is based in Orangeville, ON Canada. Currently, I am working as a freelance designer under the BLADO Design brand.
In 2021, I graduated from the Graphic Design program at Niagara College, in ON, Canada. I’ve had international work experience, in Brazil, China, and Canada. I am originally from São Paulo, Brazil, I’ve moved to Shenzhen, China, to finish high school and now Ontario, Canada. I am fluent in English and Portuguese, I also speak casual Mandarin and Spanish.
